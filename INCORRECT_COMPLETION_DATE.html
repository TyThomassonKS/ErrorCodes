How to Fix This Error

  • Review the expected completion date in column L.
  • Verify the effective risk rating and its corresponding expected duration.
  • Ensure the scheduled date aligns with the expected duration from the start date.
  • Adjust the date if necessary and re-run the validation script.
  • Ensure all dates are in valid Excel date format.
