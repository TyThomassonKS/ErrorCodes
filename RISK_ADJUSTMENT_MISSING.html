Risk Adjustment Missing

POA&M ID:

Row:

Effective Risk is marked as "Yes" or "Pending", but no adjustment has been made to the schedule.

How to Fix This Error

  • Review the "Effective Risk" column for this POA&M entry.
  • If marked "Yes" or "Pending", ensure the Scheduled Completion Date reflects the adjusted timeline.
  • Use the appropriate expected duration based on the updated risk level.
  • Update the date and re-run the validation script.
